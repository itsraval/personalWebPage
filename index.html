Alessandro Ravizzotti

In 2024 I graduated at Edinburgh Napier University and I have been awarded the Master of Science with Distinction in Advanced Security & Digital Forensics (NCSC Certified). My master's dissertation topic was regarding Behavioural Analysis of Current Evolution Ransomware Attack Exfiltration Methods. During my time at Edinburgh Napier University I have worked as Teaching Assistant for the MSc level module Computer Penetration Testing.

In 2022 I graduated at the University of Milan and I have been awarded the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I participated in the first Italian training program in cyber security called CyberChallenge and qualified among the six members of the University of Milan's team for the national competition.

I am passionate about cybersecurity and my favourite research topics regards Penetration Testing, Cryptography and Digital Forensics. I like to do CTF and A/D challenges and develop scripts to solve problems and make life easier with my favourite programming languages: Python, Javascript, C and Java.
